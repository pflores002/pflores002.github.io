Bank Account

Our team, consisting of me, Noah Flaum, and Eowyn Adair, developed a python project of bank accounts through repl.it, allowing us to program inputs and loops efficiently.

We have had some difficulties that had to do with a slight error in grammar or a complete error in the formatting of the code, but they were fairly simple fixes that needed changes from one section of code to another or fixing a word.

This project was mostly collaborative, even though it became more independent towards the end, and each one of us did have a sort of role; my role was to suggest certain codes such as error inputs or fix slight grammar errors.
