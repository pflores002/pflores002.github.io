Resume

My objective is to become an animator and create shows or visual effects for future movies.

I live in Palm Desert, CA and I am currently studying at Palm Desert High School. I have been studying at Palm Desert High School for almost 4 years and I have enrolled into courses such as computer science, art, and film. There are multiple colleges that I wish to attend such as California State University- Fullerton, California State University- Long Beach, Savannah College of Art and Design, Laguna College of Art and Design, Academy of Art University, and CalArts. All of which I am interested in their animation prograpms as a major and, for the CSU colleges, astronomy as a minor.

A work experience that I have had was mainly in film, I assisted my friends in their series, Under the Waves, in which I got to work with the camera. I also edited a horror film that we recorded at the Coachella Valley Movie Ranch as well as storyboarded the film.

In my years of high school I have achieved two certificates for Principal's Honor Roll in 2017 and 2018, which is given to those with a 3.0 GPA or higher. I enrolled in many honors and AP classes over the years, but currently I am taking AP Literature and Composition and AP Studio Art. I used to be enrolled in honors Spanish and I am fairly fluent in the language- mostly I am able to understand words,not quite able to speak it. I am also enrolled in film, and I would edit and storyboard films though recently I have had the chance to be a director and screenwrite a film. In addition to film, I am also in art. I draw traditionally with a pencil, but I have explored other mediums such as charcoal, and painted with oil and acryllic. I also draw digitally with Adobe Sketch.
